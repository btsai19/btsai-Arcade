How to Play
Controls
  • →
    Click the typing area and start typing. Press Space after each word.
  • ⌫
    Backspace corrects letters in the current word only.
  • ↹
    Tab = instant restart. Esc = back to menu.
Display Modes
  • ¶
    Paragraph — words flow in a continuous stream. Best for high WPM flow state.
  • W
    Word — one word at a time, large and centered. Next words previewed below.
Modes
  • ⚡
    Sprint — type as many words as possible before time runs out.
  • 💀
    Sudden Death — one wrong letter ends your run immediately.
  • 🌊
    Cascade — words fall from above. Space/Enter to destroy. Three lives.
