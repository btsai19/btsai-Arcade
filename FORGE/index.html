How to Play
1
Tap a tile
Select any tile on the grid — it highlights with an orange ring.
2
Tap an adjacent match
Tap a neighbour (up, down, left, or right) of the same tier to merge them into the next tier up. Only adjacent tiles can merge.
3
New tiles spawn
After each merge, 1–2 new low-tier tiles appear near existing ones. The grid fills gradually.
4
Reach The Forge
Merge through all 8 tiers before the grid seals with no moves left.
