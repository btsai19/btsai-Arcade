HOW TO PLAY
The Basics
1
On your turn, play cards from your hand (optional) to mess with other players, then draw a card from the deck.
2
If you draw a 💣 BOOM card, you're eliminated — unless you play a 🛡️ DEFUSE card!
3
When you defuse, choose where to reinsert the bomb into the deck. Strategize — and good luck.
4
The last player standing wins. Use action cards to force others to draw, steal their cards, or shuffle the deck.
Card Reference
