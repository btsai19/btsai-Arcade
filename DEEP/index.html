HOW TO PLAY
1
CLICK TO REVEAL
Left-click any cell to reveal it. Empty cells cascade automatically to all connected safe neighbours.
2
FLAG DEPTH CHARGES
Right-click (or long-press on mobile) to place an amber beacon on a cell you suspect hides a depth charge.
3
READ THE NUMBERS
Each number shows how many depth charges are hidden in the 8 surrounding cells. Use them to deduce safe cells.
4
PRESSURE MODE
The tide rises every 12 seconds, auto-revealing a row from the bottom. Move faster or the ocean decides for you.
